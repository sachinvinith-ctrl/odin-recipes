Cheese burger


Description

Cheese burger is a hamburger that is topped with fine melted cheese, served on a bun with various condiments and toppings like lettuce, tomato, pickles and onions, making it a popular and versatile meal with many variations

Ingredients

  • Groundmeat
  • Cheese
  • Bun

Steps

  1. Season the pattu(salt, pepper, garlic/onion powder) and cook
  2. Place cheese on the patty as it finishes cooking to melt
  3. Assemble the bun with sauce, patty/cheese, and desired toppings
