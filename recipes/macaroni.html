Macaroni


Description

Macaroni, known in italian as maccheroni, is a pasta shaped like shallow tubes. Made with durun wheat, macaroni is commonly cut in short lengths and served.

Ingredients

  • macaroni pasta
  • Water
  • Salt
  • creamy cheddar cheese

Steps

  1. Boil the water: In a large pot or kettle, bring the water to a rolling boil.
  2. Add pasta: Add the dry macaroni and salt to the boiling water and stir immediately for about 30 seconds to prevent sticking.
  3. Cook: Cook uncovered for approximately 6-11 minutes, or until the pasta is tender (al dente), stirring occasionally. The exact time may vary, so check your pasta package directions.
  4. Drain: Remove from heat and drain the water. You can rinse the pasta after draining if desired, but this is optional.
  5. Boil the water: In a large pot or kettle, bring the water to a rolling boil.
  6. Combine: Return the drained pasta to the saucepan. Stir in the cheese sauce and heat over medium heat, stirring often, until the mixture is hot.
